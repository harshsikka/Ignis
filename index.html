IGNIS is an early stage, international cooperative fund reaching toward future markets and world conditions.

We back bold, visionary entrepreneurs who like to thing big and move fast. Those that truly care about shipping value in new novel ways. If you haven't heard of them yet, you will soon.

Our investment portfolio, while young, is incredibly diverse. Current Investments include companies that are solving the world's big issues from renewable energy to accessible learning, and everything in between.

In addition to funding, we provide entrepreneurs with access to expertise and insights in innovation, executive and technical talent, market intelligence, policy and regulatory affairs, business development, and brand-building.
